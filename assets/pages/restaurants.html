Our Featured Restaurants

Kamala Cafe

Welcome to Kamala Cafe in Tanay!

Kamala Cafe, a delightful Thai restaurant nestled in the heart of Cuyambay, Tanay, opened its doors in 2022, quickly becoming a culinary gem for both locals and travelers alike. The restaurant offers an authentic Thai dining experience, drawing inspiration from the rich and diverse flavors of Thai cuisine.

Situated amidst the picturesque surroundings of Tanay, Kamala Cafe provides a tranquil escape for diners looking to savor delectable Thai dishes in a serene ambiance. The restaurant's warm and inviting atmosphere, coupled with its tastefully decorated interiors, creates a welcoming space for guests to enjoy a memorable dining experience.

Kamala Cafe has gained popularity not only among the local community but also among travelers from within and outside Metro Manila. Its strategic location makes it a convenient stop for those exploring the scenic beauty of Tanay or en route to other destinations. The restaurant caters to the diverse tastes of its patrons, offering a menu that showcases a wide range of Thai culinary delights, from flavorful curries to aromatic stir-fried dishes.

The chefs at Kamala Cafe skillfully craft each dish using authentic Thai ingredients, ensuring an experience that transports diners to the streets of Bangkok. The restaurant's commitment to quality, combined with its dedication to providing exceptional service, has earned it a reputation as a go-to destination for Thai cuisine enthusiasts.

Whether one is a local resident seeking a culinary adventure or a traveler eager to indulge in the exotic flavors of Thailand, Kamala Cafe stands as a beacon of Thai gastronomy in Tanay, promising a dining experience that tantalizes the taste buds and leaves a lasting impression on all who visit.

Wyndell Al Fresco-Tanay

Welcome to Wyndell Al Fresco in Tanay!

Nestled amidst the scenic landscapes of Tanay, Rizal, Wyndell Al Fresco offers a serene escape for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty. Our establishment stands as a haven where modern comfort meets the splendor of nature, providing a refreshing retreat from the bustling city life.

Discover our cozy and inviting accommodations thoughtfully designed to immerse you in the surrounding beauty while ensuring your comfort and relaxation. Each room is a peaceful sanctuary, offering scenic views of the lush countryside or the majestic mountains, allowing you to unwind and rejuvenate in style.

Indulge in a culinary journey at our restaurant, where delectable flavors and locally sourced ingredients come together to create an unforgettable dining experience. Delight your taste buds with a variety of dishes, inspired by both traditional Filipino flavors and international cuisines, all prepared with care and served in a warm and inviting ambiance.

Experience the abundance of nature's offerings as you explore the nearby attractions and activities. From hiking trails and nature walks to nearby waterfalls and cultural sites, Wyndell Al Fresco serves as the perfect starting point for your outdoor adventures.

Our commitment to exceptional service ensures that every guest feels welcomed and cared for throughout their stay. Whether you're here for a relaxing getaway, an adventure-filled holiday, or a peaceful retreat, our dedicated team is here to ensure your stay is memorable and enjoyable.

Come and immerse yourself in the natural wonders of Tanay while experiencing the comfort and hospitality of Wyndell Al Fresco—an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a tranquil escape

TANTO

Experience the Essence of Japan at Tanto Taste of Asia!

Indulge in an unforgettable culinary journey as we invite you to savor the authentic flavors of Japan all week long. Immerse yourself in the rich and diverse cuisine of Japan, right here at Tanto.

Throughout the week, embark on a tantalizing exploration of Japanese gastronomy, meticulously crafted by our expert chefs. Delight your senses with the refined elegance of sushi and sashimi, the comforting warmth of ramen, the crispy perfection of tempura, the umami richness of yakitori, and the artistry of delicate, hand-rolled maki.

From Monday to Sunday, our special menu showcases an array of Japanese specialties, ensuring that each day offers a new and exciting culinary experience. Join us as we pay homage to the culinary traditions and flavors that define the unique and captivating taste of Japan.

At Tanto Taste of Asia, our commitment to excellence extends beyond the dishes we serve. Prepare to be immersed in an ambiance that reflects the cultural richness of Japan, complemented by attentive service and a welcoming atmosphere.

Whether you're a seasoned lover of Japanese cuisine or eager to explore its nuances, our curated selection promises to satisfy discerning palates and leave you craving for more.

Join us throughout the week and immerse yourself in the exquisite taste of Japan at Tanto Taste of Asia—an experience that embodies the essence of Japanese culinary artistry and hospitality.
