The Sierra Madre Resort and Hotel

Welcome to The Sierra Madre Resort and Hotel

Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of the Sierra Madre mountain range, our resort and hotel offer an unparalleled escape into nature's embrace. Situated in Tanay Rizal, our serene retreat is designed to provide guests with an exquisite blend of luxury, tranquility, and adventure.

Accommodations at The Sierra Madre Resort and Hotel are crafted to offer a comfortable and rejuvenating stay. From cozy rooms to spacious suites, each appointed with modern amenities and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains or lush greenery, we ensure a peaceful sanctuary for relaxation and indulgence.

Indulge in an array of exquisite dining experiences curated by our award-winning chefs. From the tantalizing flavors of local Cebuano cuisine to international gastronomic delights, our diverse culinary offerings promise an unforgettable culinary journey. Savor fresh seafood at Ocean Pavilion, enjoy fine dining at Acqua, or unwind with cocktails at our beachfront bar while witnessing the mesmerizing sunset.

Our resort features a range of amenities and activities for every type of traveler. Immerse yourself in the refreshing waters of our pool, embark on nature trails and hikes, or unwind with a massage at our spa—the choice is yours. For the adventurous souls, a plethora of outdoor excursions and activities await, including mountain biking, bird watching, and guided tours through the scenic wonders of the Sierra Madre.

Indulge your taste buds at our onsite restaurant, where a delightful fusion of local flavors and international cuisine awaits. Enjoy a dining experience surrounded by the beauty of nature, complemented by attentive service and an inviting ambiance.

At The Sierra Madre Resort and Hotel, our dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every guest's stay is memorable and enjoyable.

Whether you seek a peaceful retreat, an adventurous getaway, or a venue for special occasions, our resort and hotel offer a unique opportunity to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of daily life and reconnect with nature's splendor.

Come and experience the tranquility and beauty of The Sierra Madre Resort and Hotel—an oasis of serenity nestled within the majestic Sierra Madre mountain range.
